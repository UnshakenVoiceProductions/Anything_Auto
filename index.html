Frequently asked questions

Simple answers for common first-time customer questions.

Can I ask a question before I schedule?

Yes. Start with Ask Chris. If you still need direct help, the question can be sent for personal follow-up.

Do I need to know exactly what is wrong with my vehicle?

No. Plain-language descriptions are enough. Include the symptoms, when they happen, and any warning lights or noises.

Can I request an inspection appointment online?

Yes. Use the service request form and include any concerns that may affect inspection or repair planning.

Will more customer tools be added later?

Yes. The launch version stays clear and straightforward while leaving room for future customer features.
